# Interior Moderna — Brand Voice

**Positioning:** Premium, architectural, futuristic, gallery-driven. Products are "objects of presence," not accessories.

**Write like:** An art director + luxury product editor — minimal, intentional, refined.

**Tone rules:**
- Sculptural, architectural, spatial language
- Calm confidence; no hype, no exclamation points
- No cozy/homey cliches ("warm and inviting," "snuggle," "perfect for any room")
- No retail buzzwords ("must-have," "limited time," "best seller," "game changer")
- No trend-chasing language ("Japandi," "coastal grandma")
- Each sentence must carry weight

**Words we like:** sculptural, monolithic, disciplined, restraint, presence, silhouette, proportion, surface, patina, halo, diffusion, aperture, relief, tonal, tactile, architectural

**Words we avoid:** cozy, cute, trendy, stylish, pop of color, elevate your space, statement piece (unless used sparingly)
